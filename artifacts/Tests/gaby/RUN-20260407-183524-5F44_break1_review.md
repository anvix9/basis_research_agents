- [2023] **Mechanization and sustainable agri-food system transformation in the Global South. A review** — Synthesized mechanization's complex sustainability implications, moving beyond productivity to multi-dimensional assessment
- [2023] **A Systematic Review of Agricultural Sustainability Indicators** — Most recent comprehensive synthesis of sustainability indicator research, documenting the state of measurement science
- [2023] **Concepts of agricultural marginal lands and their utilisation: A review** — Demonstrated that 'marginal' is historically contingent, not fixed — relevant to understanding how scale and viability are defined
