The complete absence of religious studies engagement is particularly notable given the field's expertise in analyzing violence against religious communities and institutions.
  *Religious analysis would enrich understanding of scholasticide's cultural meaning but is not required for legal classification, which relies on IHL and ICL frameworks rather than religious law*
